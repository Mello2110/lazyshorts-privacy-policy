Privacy Policy

Overview

LazyShorts is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed with privacy as a core principle.

No Data Collection No Analytics 100% Offline

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. LazyShorts operates entirely offline and does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Use analytics or tracking scripts
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Store any information outside your browser

100% Offline Functionality

LazyShorts works completely offline. All features are executed locally in your browser without any network requests. Your viewing habits and preferences stay on your device.

Required Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

storage
Used to save your preferences and settings locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device.
activeTab
Allows the extension to interact with the currently active tab when you click the extension icon.
youtube.com (Host Permission)
Required to apply the extension's functionality on YouTube pages. The extension only activates on YouTube and does not access any other websites.

Third-Party Services

LazyShorts does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, advertisers, or analytics platforms.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date below. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
